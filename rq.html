Research Questions on Federated Learning in 6G Networks and IoT

  • Federated Learning for 6G Networks:
    Research Questions:
    • What specific device parameters (e.g., battery level, processing power) and network conditions (e.g., latency, bandwidth) most significantly impact the performance and efficiency of federated learning systems?
    • How can context-aware decision-making be effectively integrated into federated learning frameworks to optimize resource allocation and communication strategies based on real-time environmental conditions?
    • What mechanisms can be developed to enable real-time dynamic adjustments of communication frequency and model update intervals based on fluctuating device capabilities and network conditions in federated learning?
    • What specific strategies can be implemented to monitor and manage energy consumption during the federated learning process, particularly for devices with limited battery capacity?
    • What tailored techniques can be employed to minimize communication overhead in federated learning systems while ensuring the integrity and performance of the global model?
    • What framework can be established to develop comprehensive sustainability metrics that accurately assess the environmental impact of federated learning, including energy efficiency and resource utilization?
    • What lightweight encryption techniques can be designed to enhance data privacy and security in federated learning without significantly increasing computational and communication overhead?
    • How can federated learning frameworks be tailored to effectively manage the heterogeneity of participating devices, ensuring fair resource allocation and participation from devices with varying capabilities?
    • In what specific ways can the unique features of 6G networks (e.g., ultra-reliable low-latency communication) be utilized to enhance the adaptability and scalability of federated learning applications?
    • What adaptations are necessary in federated learning algorithms to address the unique challenges posed by specific applications, such as smart agriculture or massive machine-type communication (mMTC)?
    • What robust methodologies can be employed to evaluate and validate the performance of the proposed dynamic adaptive framework in practical 6G scenarios, compared to existing federated learning approaches?
